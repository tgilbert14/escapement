The Escapement

A mechanical watch the size of a room, keeping your actual time through a real gear train.

  • The crown, right flank — hold to wind. W
    She runs down if you leave her. She remembers.
  • The slide, left flank — pull and let go: the gongs chime the hour, the quarters, the minutes. R
  • The pushers — chronograph. C start · X reset
  • Plan lifts the movement into its eight layers, still running. P
  • Pinch or scroll to lean in. + − 0, arrows to look around.
  • After dark the lume comes up — and the moon in the barrel is the real one.
